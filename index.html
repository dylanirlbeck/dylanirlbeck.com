Hi, I'm Dylan.

I'm currently a software engineer at Flexport, where I build tools to make global trade easy for everyone. I'm also a proud alumni and recent graduate of the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). When able, I spend time thinking about the intersection of technology and society (particularly around computing ethics), civic and government tech, and the philosophy of science and engineering.

Previously, I have worked on improving internal documentation with the General Services Adminstration (as a Coding it Forward Civic Digital Fellow); advocated for -- and began building -- a more robust computing ethics curriculum at UIUC; and developed software for a number of Chicago startups and organizations.
