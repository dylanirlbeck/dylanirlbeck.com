Books

In a dual order of recency and preference, here are all the books I've read or am currently reading. Within each year, I present the books by the rough order in which I most enjoyed them. You'll notice that there are a substantial number of books in 2021: this is when I'm starting off my record, so the books listed there could have been read anytime up to and including that year.

When linking to a book, I attempt to avoid any particular seller (especially Amazon). If this isn't feasible, I'll default to an independent bookseller or no link at all. In addition, I try to reflect -- in varying degrees of detail -- on every book I read. I link to these reflections when they're available.

Books I'm currently reading are highlighted in light blue.
